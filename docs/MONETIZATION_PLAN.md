# Life OS Monetization Plan

## Positioning

Life OS is an AI chief of staff for people who need their plans to chase them back. The wedge is aggressive follow-through: time-blocked planning, phone reminders, snooze escalation, and AI recovery when the user slips.

## Target Users

- Students with chaotic schedules.
- Solo founders and creators.
- ADHD-adjacent productivity users who need external pressure.
- Fitness / habit transformation users.
- Knowledge workers trying to protect deep work.

## Pricing

- Free: local-first planning, goals, basic habits, manual scheduling.
- Pro at $9/month: AI planning, automatic schedule generation, push escalation, weekly review, cloud sync.
- Max at $19/month: unlimited AI, proactive interventions, calendar integration, advanced analytics.

## Launch Offer

- First 100 users: $49 lifetime founder plan.
- Use this to validate demand before building heavy billing complexity.

## Conversion Flow

1. Landing page promises: "An AI planner that actually nags you until you do it."
2. User creates first goal.
3. AI converts goal into the next 7 days.
4. User enables push reminders.
5. After first reminder loop works, prompt upgrade: "Unlock automatic weekly planning and proactive recovery."

## MVP Paid Features

- AI weekly planning.
- Auto time-blocking.
- Escalating notifications.
- Cloud sync.
- Weekly review.

## Growth Loops

- Shareable weekly recap image: "I completed 23 focused blocks this week."
- Public challenge templates: "30-day deep work sprint", "12 books this year", "summer cut plan".
- Referral: invite a friend, both get one month Pro.

## Metrics

- Activation: user creates a goal and accepts an AI schedule.
- Habit hook: at least one notification action response.
- Retention: 3 active days in first week.
- Revenue: trial-to-paid conversion after first weekly review.
- North star: completed time-blocks per active user per week.

## Build Order

1. Reliable reminder loop.
2. Cloud sync and auth.
3. Landing/pricing.
4. Stripe checkout.
5. Weekly review.
6. Calendar integration.
7. App store wrapper.
